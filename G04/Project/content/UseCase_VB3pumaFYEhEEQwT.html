Use Case - #1: Order Meal link

Name Value
Description

A Patron accesses the Cafeteria Ordering System from either the corporate intranet or external Internet, views the menu for a specifc date, selects food items, and places an order for a meal to be picked up in the cafeteria or delivered to a specifed location within a specifed 15-minute time window.

Scenario
Steps
1. 1. Patron asks to view menu for a specifc date. (see 1.0.E1, 1.0.E2)
2. SYSTEM displays menu of available food items and the daily special.
3. 3. Patron selects one or more food items from menu. (see 1.1)
4. 4. Patron indicates that meal order is complete. (see 1.2)
5. 5. COS displays ordered menu items, individual prices, and total price, including taxes and delivery charge.
6. 6. Patron either confirms meal order (continue normal ﬂow) or requests to modify meal order (return to step 2).
7. 7. COS displays available delivery times for the delivery date.
8. 8. Patron selects a delivery time and specifes the delivery location.
9. 9. Patron specifes payment method.
10. 10. COS confrms acceptance of the order.
11. 11. COS sends Patron an email message confrming order details, price, and delivery instructions.
12. 12. COS stores order, sends food item information to Cafeteria Inventory System, and updates available delivery times.

Preconditions PRE-1. Patron is logged into COS. PRE-2. Patron is registered for meal payments by payroll deduction.
Post-conditions POST-1. Meal order is stored in COS with a status of “Accepted.” POST-2. Inventory of available food items is updated to reﬂect items in this order. POST-3. Remaining delivery capacity for the requested time window is updated.
